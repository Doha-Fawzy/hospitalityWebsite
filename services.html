Our Services

Integrated Facilities Management Scope

Bright Dimension Establishment (bood) offers integrated facility management services that combine Soft Services, Hard Services, and environmental and operational services, ensuring the client has a single capable entity to manage site requirements efficiently and transparently.

Soft Services

Improving the facility environment and elevating the level of cleanliness, comfort, and general impression.

  • General, periodic, and deep cleaning services
  • Cleaning offices, corridors, halls, and restrooms
  • Hospitality and operational support services
  • Safe and organized waste management and sorting
  • Pest control according to approved schedules

Hard Services

Maintaining asset readiness, reducing breakdowns, and increasing equipment lifespan.

  • Preventive, corrective, and emergency maintenance
  • Electrical systems and plumbing network maintenance
  • HVAC systems maintenance
  • General repairs inside facilities
  • Tracking breakdown reports and work orders

Landscape & Agriculture

Creating a sustainable aesthetic environment and preserving the quality of green spaces.

  • Seasonal irrigation programs based on weather
  • Designing and organizing gardens and green areas
  • Planting trees, shrubs, and ornamental plants
  • Safe agricultural pest control
  • Soil fertilization and plant growth improvement

ManWinWin CMMS

Digital system for maintenance and asset management.

  • Accurate recording and tracking of work orders
  • Efficient preventive maintenance scheduling
  • Generating instant, reliable reports
  • Transitioning from reactive to proactive maintenance
  • Managing spare parts and analyzing breakdown data

ISO Standards & BICSc Compliance

We are committed to applying work methodologies compatible with relevant international standards for Quality, Occupational Health & Safety, Environmental Management, and Facilities Management.

ISO 9001 ISO 45001 ISO 14001 ISO 41001 BICSc Certified
